Why a Digital Portfolio?

There are multitudes of benefits that come with creating a Digital Portfolio.

First, and most obvious, is its easy access and superb organization. All of one's projects can be stored right in the Digital Portfolio, making it a great way to display a person's talents, accomplishments, and creations. A digital portfolio can even be used to get jobs because many employers like seeing one's knowledge all in one place.

A second, and maybe more obscure, benefit is the ability to track one's growth overtime. With a Digital Portfolio, a person can easily look back at their past work and compare it to their new ones. This can lead to a greater understanding of where you have already improved, and where you can still improve in the future.
